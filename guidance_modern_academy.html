How to Apply

Simple Admission Process

We have made the admission process simple and transparent. Follow these 4 easy steps to secure a seat.

1
Enquiry & Visit

Visit our campus at Keshobpur or call us to learn about available seats, programs and facilities.

2
Fill Application

Collect the admission form from school office. Fill all details and attach required documents.

3
Interaction

A brief, friendly interaction with the student and parents conducted by our principal.

4
Confirmation

Pay the admission fee and receive your admission confirmation and school ID card.

📋 Documents Required
Birth Certificate · Previous School TC (if applicable) · 4 Passport Photos · Parent/Guardian Aadhaar · Residence Proof · Caste Certificate (if applicable)
